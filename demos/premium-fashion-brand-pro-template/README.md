# XYZ — Premium Fashion Brand Template

A premium black-and-white fashion / streetwear website template by Twinstinct Digital.

## Sections included
- Cinematic hero
- Sticky glass navbar
- Mobile menu
- Product collection cards
- New drop product cards
- Brand story
- Editorial lookbook
- Reviews
- Newsletter CTA
- Contact + WhatsApp CTA
- Custom cursor
- Scroll reveal animations
- Scroll progress bar
- Responsive design

## How to customize
1. Replace XYZ with client brand name.
2. Change WhatsApp number in index.html.
3. Replace Unsplash image URLs with client product photos.
4. Update product names, prices, sizes, and categories.
5. Upload to hosting and send live preview to client.

Suggested industries:
- Clothing brand
- Streetwear startup
- Fashion boutique
- Sneaker store
- Luxury apparel brand
